Backpacking With A Baby

Here is a little information about our experience backpacking Italy with a 9 month old baby

We started our trip with a direct flight from Vancouver to London. We visited family for about a week before venturing off on our own for the Italian backpacking trip of a lifetime. While our days of 12 person hostel rooms may be over, we wanted to show people that just because you have kids, doesn't mean you cant travel. We covered a lot of ground and made memories that will last forever during our month in Italia.

Places We Travelled

  • Sicily
  • Naples
  • Rome
  • Florence
  • Pisa
  • Cinque Terre
  • Venice
